Delicious meat sauce recipe!

Italian style meat sauce, just like your grandma used to make!

This incredibly rich meat sauce recipe is one that has been passed down for 7 generations.

This has allowed a perfect mix of old and contemporary influences to work its magic on this mouth watering recipe. Try it and see for yourself!

    Ingredients:
  • 400g ground beef
  • 200g italian sausage
  • One tablespoon basil
  • One tablespoon oregano
  • half teaspoon of salt
  • quarter teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 400g can of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoon of tomato paste
